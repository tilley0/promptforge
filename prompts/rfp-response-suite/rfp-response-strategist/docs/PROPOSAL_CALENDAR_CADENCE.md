## Proposal Phases & Milestones

### Phase 0 – Kickoff & Readiness
- GO / NO-GO decision
- Compliance matrix initiation
- Evaluation factor extraction
- Teaming decision
**Exit Criteria:** Drafting authorized

---

### Phase 1 – Compliance & Solution Framing
- Compliance matrix ≥ 80%
- Solution outline approved
- Staffing model validated
- Pricing assumptions aligned
**Exit Criteria:** Volume drafting begins

---

### Phase 2 – Draft Development
- Initial drafts per volume complete
- Integration pass #1
- Compliance traceability verified
**Exit Criteria:** Red Team ready

---

### Phase 3 – Reviews & Hardening
- Red Team review
- Kill Shot review
- Time-boxed rewrite
- Integration pass #2
**Exit Criteria:** Final draft stable

---

### Phase 4 – Finalization & Submission
- Final compliance check
- Final price realism check
- Executive approval
- Submission dry run
**Exit Criteria:** Submitted

---

## Standing Cadence (Recommended)

### Daily (15–30 min)
- Status vs schedule
- New blockers
- Amendment impacts

### Weekly (or Phase Gate)
- Compliance status
- Risk review
- Teaming/pricing changes

### Review Events (Mandatory)
- Red Team (scored)
- Kill Shot (binary survival)
- Final Compliance Audit

---

## Review Freeze Rules
- No new content after Kill Shot review
- Fixes only (no new claims)
- Pricing changes must trigger re-verification

---

## Roles & Accountability
- **Proposal Manager:** Schedule, decisions, escalation
- **Compliance Lead:** Binary compliance authority
- **Integrator:** Cross-volume consistency
- **Volume Leads:** Content quality
- **Red Team:** Evaluator simulation

Undefined ownership = missed deadlines.
